About the Bees

We rescue our bees from locations around North Carolina where the bees are in danger or endangering the public. The North Carolina honey bee population is extremely important to wildlife. So far, we have saved 10+ bee colonies around North Carolina. That's nearly 10,000 bees! Each store location specializes in a different bee product flavor. We also have unflavored products that are sold at every location.

Boone

Our Boone location was established in 2016. This location specializes in clover flavored bee products.

Chapel Hill

Our Chapel Hill location was established in 2018. This location specializes in rosemary flavored bee products.

Louisburg

Our Louisburg location was our first location, established in 2008. This location specializes in honeysuckle flavored bee products.

Charlotte

Our Charlotte location was established in 2015. This location specializes in lavendar flavored bee products.
